Fans in Baltimore were treated to a Prince concert last week at Royal Farms Arena.
The purple one hasn't performed in Baltimore in 14 years, but was in top form during the "Rally 4 Peace" show, that promoted healing after weeks of rioting following the death of Freddie Gray, who died last month after being arrested by city officers.

Fans sang along with Prince and yelled "no curfew" between songs.
Partial proceeds from the concert will be donated to Baltimore youth charities.
